About

Game Programmer & Systems Architect

I am a Game Programmer specializing in gameplay systems and tools development, primarily working with Unity and C#. My programming approach focuses on building modular, scalable systems that remain engine and language agnostic. This flexibility allows me to adapt quickly to new technologies and environments, applying a structured problem-solving methodology regardless of the specific tools in use.

Engineering Philosophy

I value clean architecture, readable code, and systems designed for long-term maintainability. Whether I am navigating standard development cycles or high-intensity production periods, my goal is to remain efficient and organized. I strive to build robust systems that integrate seamlessly with the broader project ecosystem.

Modern Development Workflow

I actively integrate AI into my development process as a focused assistant rather than a replacement for core engineering. I utilize these tools to:

  • Assist with small, specific implementations.
  • Explore alternative architectural approaches.
  • Test formulas and simulate outcomes in diverse scenarios.

While this portfolio was refined with the help of AI, all design decisions, architectural structures, and core implementations are my own. I view AI as a powerful accelerant that enhances effective development, though I remain fully capable of delivering high-quality results without its assistance.

Interdisciplinary Interests

Beyond the core logic of programming, I have a deep-seated interest in the holistic elements of game creation, including:

  • Cinematography: Exploring the visual language of interactive media.
  • Game Design: Understanding the synergy between mechanics, narrative, and visual presentation to create memorable user experiences.
